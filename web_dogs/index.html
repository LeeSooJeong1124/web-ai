All About Dogs

Dogs, also known as domestic dogs, are domesticated canids belonging to the species Canis lupus familiaris. They are beloved companions and working animals, known for their loyalty, intelligence, and diverse breeds. From tiny Chihuahuas to massive Great Danes, dogs come in a wide variety of shapes, sizes, and temperaments. They have been human companions for thousands of years, providing companionship, protection, and assistance in various tasks such as hunting, herding, and search and rescue.
