Welcome to the World of Birds

Birds are a group of warm-blooded vertebrates constituting the class Aves, characterized by feathers, toothless beaked jaws, the laying of hard-shelled eggs, a high metabolic rate, a four-chambered heart, and a strong yet lightweight skeleton. They are found worldwide and in all habitats. Explore our gallery and video to see some of these magnificent creatures.
